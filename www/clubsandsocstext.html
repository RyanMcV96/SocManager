Stirling University offers a wide selection of clubs and societies, it is easy to be overwhelmed at first. Using our SocManager it is simple to stay up to date on all your selected clubs events, socials, meeting times and news.

Simply check off the clubs you're interested in knowing more about and hit the submit button to be notified whenever there is anything new to know!
